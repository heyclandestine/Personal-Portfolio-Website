About me

    My name is Kendel Fenner and I'm a JD/MBA student at the University of Oklahoma. I was born and raised in Shreveport, Louisiana. I am currently attempting a balancing act of pursuing law, finance, and computer science. I initially intended to practice law straight out of law school, but after being exposed to the world of finance and going through enough rabbit holes, I have discovered the world of quantitative finance and decided that it's what I want to do as a career long-term.

    This website is evidence of the start of my computer science journey. With this, I have learned as much html and css as I intend to use for future projects. Next, I will be learning Javascript, and then Python, hoping to learn the fundamentals of both and gain the ability to create pet projects in the realm of quant finance. In the future, I would also love to learn C++ and delve deep into understanding computer architecture and how the machine interacts with the OS, as well as other software.

    I know that I have a long way to go to break in to this exclusive and premier field, but I know I can do it. I think I have a passion for problem solving, being challenged, and trying to understand complex systems, of which finance and computer science can offer in spades. I will try to update this page as I make more progress.
